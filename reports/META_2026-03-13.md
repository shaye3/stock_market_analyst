## 3. INDUSTRY ANALYSIS

### Digital Advertising Market

The global advertising market crosses $1 trillion for the first time in 2026 (Dentsu forecast), with digital advertising comprising the dominant and growing share. Global digital ad spend is projected at approximately $354.9 billion in 2026, growing at a CAGR of ~13.8%.

Meta operates in an advertising duopoly alongside Alphabet/Google. Together, the two companies command approximately 55-60% of global digital advertising revenue. No other platform approaches their scale, targeting precision, or measurement capabilities.

**Key Market Dynamics:**
- AI-driven ad optimization is expanding market size by improving advertiser ROI, drawing more budget from traditional media
- Retail media (shoppable ads, direct-to-consumer) is the fastest-growing adjacent category
- Video advertising, particularly AI-generated creative, is experiencing rapid format growth
- Performance marketing (measurable ROI) is structurally more resilient than brand advertising in downturns

**Meta's Competitive Position (Moats):**
1. **Social Graph:** 20+ years of identity, social connections, and interest data — impossible to replicate from scratch
2. **Scale:** 3.27B DAUs across the Family of Apps
3. **AI Targeting Infrastructure:** Advantage+ and related tools demonstrably deliver superior advertiser ROI vs. alternatives
4. **Cross-Platform Reach:** Advertisers can reach users across Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger in unified campaigns
5. **SMB Base:** 10M+ active advertisers provide diversified, sticky demand
6. **Network Effects:** More users attract more advertisers; more advertiser data improves targeting; better targeting attracts more users

**Competitive Threats:**
- TikTok: Most significant attention competitor, particularly among younger demographics; AI-native recommendation system
- Google/YouTube: Dominant in search intent-based advertising; strong video position
- Apple Intelligence: Reduces third-party data availability; threat to cross-app tracking
- OpenAI/ChatGPT: Emerging threat to information-seeking behavior

**Industry Score: POSITIVE** — Secular growth, duopoly position, defensible moats
